On-Page SEO Optimization

A Real World Case Study

Date

14 May, 2023

Client

Cspade LLc

Location

Bolingbrooke Illinois, USA

Project Overview

Cspade is an E-commerce business focused on selling african made products in the United States. the objective of our work with them was to improve the on-page SEO elements of their blog to enhance search engine visibility, organic traffic, and overall user experience.



Strategy and Execution

  • Keyword Research and Targeting

    We conducted extensive keyword research to identify relevant and high-volume keywords within the technology niche. Analyzed user intent to align content with search queries and selected primary and secondary keywords for each blog post.

  • Optimized Title Tags and Meta Descriptions

    We Updated title tags and meta descriptions of existing blog posts and optimized them with the target keywords. Ensured the tags were compelling, descriptive, and within the recommended character limits.

  • Heading Structure and Keyword Placement

    Ensured proper heading structure (H1, H2, H3, etc.) for each blog post. Incorporated target keywords naturally within headings and subheadings to improve both user experience and search engine optimization.

  • URL Optimization

    Optimized URLs to be concise, descriptive, and keyword-rich. Eliminated unnecessary parameters or numbers from the URLs and ensured they were easily readable by both users and search engines.

  • Content Optimization

    Analyzed existing blog content and made necessary adjustments to improve keyword density and ensure natural keyword integration. Focused on creating informative, in-depth, and engaging content that answered user queries and provided value.

  • Image Optimization

    Optimized images by compressing file sizes for faster page loading speed. Added descriptive alt text and relevant file names to improve accessibility and keyword association.
